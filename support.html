Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first invoice?

Tap the "+" button on the Documents tab, select "Invoice", fill in your client and item details, then tap "Create". You can preview and send your invoice via email or save it as a PDF.

How do I add my business logo?

Go to Settings > Business Profile > tap on the logo area to upload your business logo. It will appear on all your invoices automatically.

Can I sync my data across devices?

Yes! RapidInvoice uses iCloud to sync your invoices, clients, and settings across all your Apple devices. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID and have iCloud enabled for the app.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Go to Settings > Subscription to view Pro features and subscribe. Pro gives you unlimited invoices, premium templates, automatic payment reminders, and more.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > RapidInvoice. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

How do I mark an invoice as paid?

Open the invoice, tap the menu (three dots) button, and select "Mark as Paid". You can also record partial payments by tapping "Record Payment".

Can I customize my invoice template?

Yes! Go to Settings > Invoice Defaults to customize your default payment terms, notes, and other settings. Pro subscribers have access to additional premium templates.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Data Export to export your invoices and client data. You can export as CSV or PDF format for your records.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Your data is stored locally on your device and encrypted via iCloud. We do not have access to your invoice data. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

We appreciate your help! Please email us at support@rapidinvoice.app with a description of the issue and steps to reproduce it. Screenshots are helpful!
